Programming For Social Science

  • Assessment 1 - Agent Based Model (ABM)

    • A simple ABM implemented in Python, displaying 10 agents moving randomly and interacting with each other and resources within their environment by eating, collecting and storing resources and haring resources with neighbours. The implementation uses python list, matplotlib for plotting.

  • Assessment 2 - Black Death

    • Implemented in Python to show the scale of deaths in the Black Death of 1600. The plague was attributed to fleas from black rats infecting people. The model maps the death of 16 areas in London with the rats caught.
